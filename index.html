What is a Plant?

Plants are living organisms and members of the kingdom plantae that mostly covers the planet Earth. They can be seen everywhere like grass, trees, flowers, bushes, ferns, mosses, and more. 
 

What makes a plant a plant? 

Here are some basic characteristics that make a living organism a plant:

1. Most plants produce their own food through photosynthesis. 
2. Plants have a waxy layer or cuticle on their surface which provides their protection and keeps them from drying out.
3. Plants have eukaryotic cells.
4. Plants reproduce with sex cells or with spores.
