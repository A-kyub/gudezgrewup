I Grew Up With My Plants

REFLECTION

"I Grew Up With My Plant"- what a nice title for a project don't you think? By just reading it gives you chills and excitement to start it right away! Today, my plants are still hanging tight and growing strong. Taking care of a plant requires a lot of hardwork and FAITH. Don't ever give! Plants are living orginasms like us humans. We eat, drink, reproduce, and even create wates. Plants have life too that's why we need to take care and not to destroy them. Understanding how a plant work is amazing, but being able to observe them and taking care of them yourself is far more amazing. It's an experience that everyone should know. Realizing how important plants are is like realizing how important life is! Growing plants is more than cultivating and stuffs, it's also self-growing. While taking care of my plants, new knowledge arrives. I realized what lacks and how to solve it. Responsible is the word. We need to be responsible to grow plants. Like parenthood! We're the parents and the plants are our childrens. You'll worry, panic, etc., but seeing them grow strong will make you happy!
